Our Mission

Empowering Vulnerable to Overcome Dementia Back Home

What We Do


Do you have loved ones back home in Africa struggling with dementia and other mental challenges in old age? We understand that this can be a difficult and overwhelming experience for both the patient and in many cases there are no health facilities and caregivers to provide critical care needed to assist them. Our organization is dedicated to providing the support, resources, and guidance necessary to the poorest and most vulnerable to help you navigate this journey.



Our mission is to empower communities, particularly the elderly people, to access help proper mental health care to enable them lead healthier, happier, and more prosperous lives. We aim to build partnerships with local organizations and communities to ensure that the care provided are culturally appropriate, environmentally sustainable, and have a lasting impact."
